Required Documents to Upload

Tick each document when it is ready to send

14 docs

Passport (Home Page)

Required

Main page with photo and details. Valid for at least 1 year. PDF/JPG, max 5MB.

Student Photo

Required

White background, face forward, no hat/glasses. JPG/JPEG, max 200KB.

Diploma / Degree Certificate

Required

High school, Bachelor's, or Master's depending on level. If not in English/Chinese, provide notarized translation. PDF, max 5MB.

Academic Transcripts

Required

Official transcripts with all courses and grades. Must be stamped/sealed. Notarized translation if not in English/Chinese. PDF, max 5MB.

Language Certificate

Required

Chinese programs: HSK 4+ (≥210). English programs: TOEFL ≥80 / IELTS ≥6.0 / Duolingo ≥90. PDF/JPG, max 5MB.

Physical Examination Form

Required

Medical checkup using official "Foreigner Physical Examination Form". Must be issued within 6 months. PDF, max 5MB.

Non-Criminal Record (Police Certificate)

Required

Police clearance from local police station. Issued within 6 months. Notarized translation if needed. PDF, max 5MB.

Bank Statement / Certificate of Deposit

Required

Latest 6-month bank statement (apostilled). Closing balance by duration:
• 6-year programs (MBBS/BDS/Pharm-D from Pakistan): Minimum USD 15,000 (≈ PKR 4.2M) — USD 2,500/year × 6 years.
• Applies for self-funded or sponsor (include affidavit & FRC if sponsor).
PDF/JPG, max 5MB. Notarized translation if not in English/Chinese.

Application Fee Payment Receipt

Required

PKR 2,000 — Firstliners Education Consulting application fee (same for all universities). Pay via bank transfer or EasyPaisa/JazzCash — details provided by your agent. Non-refundable. JPG/PDF, max 2MB.

CV / Resume

Required

1–2 pages with personal info, education, work experience, skills, achievements. PDF or Word, max 2MB.

Recommendation Letters

Master/PhD only

2 letters from professors/associate professors. On official letterhead with signature. PDF, max 2MB each.

Study Plan / Research Proposal

Master/PhD only

Detailed plan: min 1,000 words (Master's), 2,000 words (PhD). Explain why you chose this university, your research interests, and future goals. PDF or Word, max 5MB.

Guardianship Certificate

Under 18 only

Required if under 18 on September 1st of enrollment year. Notarized, naming a legal guardian in China. PDF, max 2MB.

Existing China Visa Page

If previously visited China

Scan of the Chinese visa page from your passport. JPG/PDF, max 2MB.

Before You Submit — Final Checklist

  • All personal information matches your passport exactly
  • Your student email is active — Firstliners will forward all official documents to admissions@firstlinersabroad.cn

  • Your passport is valid for at least 1 year from today
